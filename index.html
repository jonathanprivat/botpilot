How It Works

Install. Enable. Go.

01

Install from Google Play

Download BotPilot and open it. No account creation, no sign-up, no API keys needed.

02

Enable the Accessibility Service

Grant BotPilot the Accessibility Service permission — same as screen readers use. This lets it see and tap Instagram's UI.

03

Pick a mode and set your rates

Choose Stories, Feed, Location, or Hashtag. Adjust the rate sliders for likes, GIF reacts, emoji reactions, and comment likes.

04

Hit start and put it down

BotPilot opens Instagram and works through your session automatically. Watch the live log or just let it run in your pocket.
